Hello! I am a PhD student at SUSTech, with a Master's degree from HKU. My research focuses on FPGA-based audio signal processing and RRAM-based neural network privacy protection.

I am particularly interested in exploring new architectures that integrate hardware and software for efficient and secure computation. Beyond research, I enjoy discussing emerging technologies and collaborating on innovative projects.

Research Interests

In-memory Computing

Development of computing architectures that process data directly within memory to reduce data movement and improve energy efficiency.

Neural Networks

RRAM-based implementations of neural networks with a focus on privacy preservation and security enhancement.

Hardware Differential Privacy

Novel hardware-based approaches to implement differential privacy for enhanced data security and user protection.

Memristor RRAM

Research on resistive random-access memory technologies for next-generation memory and computing applications.
